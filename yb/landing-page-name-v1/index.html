If I Were Your Angel

Each of us has a guardian angel that watches over us and brings us comfort throughout our day.

Through the beautiful illustrations and a heartwarming story, this special, little book will show you just how many creative ways your loved ones are still here and guide you with their love and care.

Family and Memories

Reading together as a family builds wonderful, lifelong memories

Do you remember storytime as a child?

Reading a book with parents or grandparents each evening, right before bed is one of the greatest memories a child can have. Keep those memories close to your heart and find yourself immersed in a beautiful story while being able to explain to your kids how those that passed can always be with us.

Share this wonderous story with your child as both of you feel the warmed in your hearts that come from remembering those we loved the most.

Meet the Author

Dan Bright

Dan Bright is a loving father, husbnd, writer and amateur wrangler of cats. Blessed with neither a riveting personality nor chiseled good looks, Dan relied on his storytelling as a way to connect with new people.

This skill eventually evolved into a passion for writting inspirational stories for his friends and loved ones.

When Dan is not being distracted by four demanding felines, he loves to golf, play volleyball and spend time with his family in Southern California.
